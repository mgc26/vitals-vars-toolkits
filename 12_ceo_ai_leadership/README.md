# CEO AI Leadership Assessment Toolkit

**Evaluating and developing healthcare executive leadership for AI transformation**

This toolkit provides evidence-based frameworks for assessing CEO readiness for AI transformation, establishing governance structures, and building leadership capacity for scaled AI adoption in healthcare organizations.

## What's Included

### 1. CEO AI Leadership Assessment (`assessments/ceo_ai_readiness.md`)
A comprehensive 5-dimension scorecard for evaluating executive AI readiness:
- Digital fluency and technical literacy
- Strategic vision and transformation track record
- Governance and organizational design
- Talent strategy and team building
- Commitment and tenure alignment

Use this to assess current leadership, evaluate CEO candidates, or identify development priorities.

### 2. Board Evaluation Criteria (`assessments/board_ceo_evaluation.md`)
Questions and evaluation criteria for boards of directors to use during:
- CEO hiring and search processes
- Annual performance reviews
- Succession planning
- Strategic planning discussions

### 3. AI Governance Framework (`guides/governance_framework.md`)
Organizational design patterns that enable AI scaling:
- Reporting structures (Chief AI Officer placement)
- Decision-making authority and budget ownership
- Cross-functional AI committees and councils
- Risk oversight and compliance structures

### 4. The Monday Test Scorecard (`assessments/monday_test.md`)
A weekly operational checklist for CEOs to demonstrate hands-on AI engagement:
- What AI projects did you personally review?
- Who did you meet with about AI strategy?
- What AI decisions did you make or unblock?
- What AI learning did you complete?

### 5. Talent Strategy Guide (`guides/talent_strategy.md`)
Building and retaining AI/analytics leadership:
- Chief AI Officer role definition and compensation
- Data science and ML engineering team structures
- Retention strategies for technical talent
- External partnership models (academia, vendors, consultants)

### 6. Leadership Development Roadmap (`guides/leadership_development.md`)
A 90-day plan for CEOs to build AI fluency:
- Technical education (courses, readings, site visits)
- Governance setup (committees, reporting, processes)
- Quick wins to build organizational confidence
- Communication and change management

## Who Should Use This Toolkit

**Healthcare Boards** evaluating CEO candidates or assessing current leadership for AI readiness

**Executive Search Firms** developing CEO profiles and evaluation criteria for healthcare clients

**Healthcare CEOs** seeking to develop their AI leadership capabilities and organizational structures

**Private Equity Investors** conducting due diligence on healthcare portfolio company leadership

**Payer Leadership** (commercial insurers, Medicare Advantage) evaluating executive talent for AI transformation

**Life Sciences Executives** assessing healthcare customer leadership capacity for AI partnerships

## How to Use This Toolkit

### For Board Members and Investors

1. Start with `assessments/board_ceo_evaluation.md` to frame your evaluation criteria
2. Use `assessments/ceo_ai_readiness.md` as a structured interview guide
3. Review `guides/governance_framework.md` to assess organizational design
4. Implement `assessments/monday_test.md` as an ongoing accountability mechanism

### For Current CEOs

1. Complete the self-assessment in `assessments/ceo_ai_readiness.md`
2. Implement governance structures from `guides/governance_framework.md`
3. Use `guides/talent_strategy.md` to build your AI leadership team
4. Follow the 90-day roadmap in `guides/leadership_development.md`
5. Track weekly progress with `assessments/monday_test.md`

### For Executive Search Professionals

1. Use `assessments/board_ceo_evaluation.md` to develop position profiles
2. Incorporate questions from `assessments/ceo_ai_readiness.md` into interview processes
3. Assess candidates' governance vision using `guides/governance_framework.md`
4. Evaluate talent strategy using `guides/talent_strategy.md`

## Evidence Base

This toolkit synthesizes research and data from:

- McKinsey healthcare AI adoption surveys (2024)
- American College of Healthcare Executives CEO turnover studies
- Harvard Medical School leadership research on AI implementation
- Institute of Medicine reports on transformational leadership
- Healthcare CEO tenure and turnover analysis (Becker's Hospital Review, 2024)
- Health system digital transformation priorities (Deloitte, BCG, 2024)

## Key Statistics Cited

- 85% of healthcare leaders say AI is a top priority (McKinsey, 2024)
- 75% lack resources or planning to deliver on AI priorities (McKinsey, 2024)
- Average hospital CEO tenure: 3.5 years (Black Book Rankings)
- Hospital CEO turnover: 16% annually for 3 consecutive years (ACHE)
- Only 13% of health systems have clear AI integration strategies (multiple surveys, 2024)
- 77% of health systems cite immature AI tools as a major barrier to adoption, even while evidence shows leadership is the primary driver of success or failure.
- 90% of health system executives (including CEOs) say AI is a high priority, but investment doesn't align with impact areas (McKinsey, 2024)
