# Economics robustness certificate

Economics is the only model-refutable plausibility gate. This table re-decides
it at the **build-favorable extreme** of each segment's committed cost bands
(BUILD at its low bound, the best alternative at its high bound). `robust_fail`
means the build loses even there, so no in-range estimate choice flips the verdict;
`close_call` means the verdict flips somewhere inside the bands and the article
discloses it.

| Segment | Verdict at midpoint | Build mid vs line | Class | Flips within bands? |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Regional IDN | FAIL | 6.04x the 15% line | robust_fail | no (robust) |
| Community hospital | FAIL | 10.80x the 15% line | robust_fail | no (robust) |
| Large integrated system | FAIL | 6.92x the 15% line | robust_fail | no (robust) |
| Greenfield cash-pay clinic | FAIL | 1.28x the 15% line | close_call | yes (close call) |

Line = best-alternative 5-yr NPV midpoint x (1 + 15%). A ratio above 1.00 is a fail; the multiple shows by how much.
